Tyler Hansbrough

"Psycho T"

North Carolina Tar Heels 2005-2009


Career Highlights and Awards:

  • 2005: McDonalds All-American
  • 2005: Parade All-American
  • 2006: ACC All-Freshman Team
  • 2006: ACC Rookie of the Year
  • 2006: Second Team All-American
  • 2006: First Team All ACC
  • 2006: USBWA National Freshman of the Year
  • 2007: First Team All-American
  • 2007: First Team All-ACC
  • 2008: National College Player of the Year
  • 2008: ACC Player of the Year
  • 2008: First Team All-ACC
  • 2008: First Team All-American
  • 2008: ACC Tournament MVP
  • 2009: First- Team All-American
  • 2009: First Team All-ACC
  • 2009: NCAA Champion


Tyler Hansbrough still holds the Tar Heels record all time in scoring with 2,872 points and second all time rebounding with 1,219 rebounds (Record broken in January 2023 by Armando Bacot)


In 2009 NBA Draft Hansbrough was selected in the 1st round 13th overall pick by the Indiana Pacers


No. 50 retired by North Carolina
